1. Decision Rationale & “Why” Data (Often Missed, Very High Value)
What’s usually collected
Architecture diagrams
Final implementations
What’s usually missed
Why a design was chosen
Why alternatives were rejected
Why this matters
Your LLM must answer:
“Why does Orion do it this way?”
“Why is this gap risky?”
“Why would Orion reject this approach?”
Without rationale data, the LLM can only describe, not reason.
Data to collect
Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
Design review comments
Trade‑off analyses
“Pros/cons” discussions
Where to find it
ADR repositories
Design review meeting notes
Senior architect emails or docs
Confluence pages titled “Decision”, “Rationale”
